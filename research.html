Research Interests

My research lies in both algebraic and analytic number theory. I am particularly interested in density problems related to integral sequences, as my PhD focuses on such problems in the theory of Lucas sequences and other related sequences. I also study arithmetic properties of sequences, such as their primitive divisors.
